Open Reader

Open Reader is a web app interface for Project Gutenberg

It provides a more user-friendly, clean, and enjoyable interface that allows the user to access Project Gutenberg's extensive library of books that have entered the public domain

What can I do with a registered account?

If you choose to register an account, you gain several features. You can add books to your personal bookshelf, create bookmarks to automatically begin reading from where you left off, and also experience a paginated, more book-like experience compared to reading books directly on Project Gutenberg. Create your account today by clicking the Register tab above!

What if I don't want to register an account?

No problem! You are still welcome to peruse the book collection and read at your will. However, you won't have access to some features as noted above.
